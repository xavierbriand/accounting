Right-sized the dev-loop gate (issue #160, F2 + part-F7). Three coordinated deliverables shipped:
(1) **risk-based lanes** — CLAUDE.md § 6 now routes every story into a Full / Reduced / Light lane
by risk surface (Core/DB/migrations → Full; infra or behavior-changing harness → Reduced; docs/process
→ Light), each fixing its Phase-0, review-agent set, commit envelope, and plan location; codified as
rule **R26**. (2) **truthful weight metric** — a new single-source `harness/lib/process-artifacts.ts`
excludes `docs/plans/`, `docs/retrospectives/`, `docs/status.d/` from `diff_loc`, so `weight_ratio` now
measures plan-vs-*shipped* code (>1.0 = plan heavier); the dead `commits` column is gone and
`docs/metrics/loop.csv` is regenerated to a 5-column schema. The fix flips maint-18 from a lying 0.64 to
26.0 and maint-01 from 0.70 to 4.68. (3) **wired trigger** — a new always-advisory `weight-ratio-heavy`
dod-check finding fires when a plan outweighs its shipped diff, never gating CI (h7's advisory-first
lesson). Lanes are not yet self-applied — h8 itself ran the full process (lanes don't exist until this
merges); h9–h11 are the first stories that self-select. The `process-artifacts.ts` shared filter (plus a
Phase-4 `countLoc` extraction) front-runs h10's single-source goal.
